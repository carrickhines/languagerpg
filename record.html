🎙️ Voice Studio

Record your own voice for the game — the best narrator a kid can have.

1. Tap 🎙️ on a line, read it out loud, tap ⏹ to stop (auto-stops at 6s).
2. Tap ▶ to check it; re-record any time.
3. Tap Download voice.js and put the file in the same folder as index.html (commit it to the repo for GitHub Pages).

Tip: record on the device the kids play on (e.g. the iPad, via the live site) for the most compatible audio. Use the built-in or a wired mic — Bluetooth headsets record in telephone quality. You can stop and come back — already recorded lines show ✅ and are kept when you download again.
